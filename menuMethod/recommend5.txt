Ingredients:
2 large eggs
4 slices bread (white or brown)
2 tbsp mayonnaise
Butter

Seasonings:
Salt (to taste)
Freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
1. Place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10-12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
2. Place the chopped eggs in a bowl, and stir in the mayonnaise. Season with salt and black pepper. Stir and serve on your favorite bread (spread with butter).
